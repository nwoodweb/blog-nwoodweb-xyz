What is MathJax?

MathJax is an open source JavaScript library designed to display mathematical notation within a HTTP(S) server, is actively maintained by the American Mathematical Society, and has recieved contributions from many academic institutions such as the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics, as well as StackExchange.

MathJax is designed to take either LaTeX , MathML, or ASCIIMath as inputs, and translate the input into a scalable vector format embedded within the webpage, allowing equations to be infinitely scalable, which is impossible if bitmap images are used.
